  • By default the Software trades on margin within your account equity (no leverage). Leverage — borrowing beyond your equity, up to about 2× — is an optional setting you can enable on accounts holding more than US$2,000. With leverage enabled, losses can exceed the cash you deposited, and you may owe your broker more than you put in.

Crypto assets are highly volatile, trade 24/7, and can lose most or all of their value very quickly; forex and other margin products can move sharply against you.
