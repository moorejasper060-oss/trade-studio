What's new in Trade Studio — new strategies, improvements and fixes. Newest first.

June 2026 · v1.2.18
Accuracy

Dashboard numbers you can trust

We went through every figure on the dashboard to make sure each one tells the truth. A trade that closes exactly flat is now counted as a break-even — neither a win nor a loss — so your win rate reflects real winners instead of being nudged up by scratch trades. The closed-trades list now shows why each crypto position was exited — a stop-loss, a take-profit, or a trend change — instead of labelling every exit the same way.

The crypto and forex screens now show real risk readouts — how far you are into your drawdown limit and whether new buys are allowed — and the drawdown gauge fills in step with your actual limit. A "today" chart shows only today's activity rather than slipping in older history, an offline bot is now clearly marked offline instead of looking paused, and forex accounts are shown in their own currency (€, £, ¥…) rather than always in dollars. None of this changes how your bots trade — only how honestly the screen reports what they're doing.

June 2026 · v1.2.17
Schedule + plans

Trading hours in your own time — and free strategy choice

The trading schedule now speaks your language. Set the days and hours a bot is allowed to open new trades in your own local time — Trade Studio shows you when the US market is open in your timezone and quietly converts everything to market time behind the scenes, so there's no more working out what "9:30 ET" means where you live. Exits and stop-losses always run, regardless of the schedule.

We've also simplified the plans: choosing your strategy and preset is now free on every plan — Classic or VWAP Reclaim, with the Patient, Balanced and Aggressive presets, on paper and live alike. The trading schedule and the auto-pause risk limits are now included with any paid plan. And the per-bot leverage toggle now confirms clearly the moment it switches on or off.

A more reliable Configure panel — and updates that find you

The ⚙ Configure panel on each bot now opens reliably every time. We also added a gentle banner that appears the moment a new version is ready to install, so you no longer need to open Settings to notice an update is waiting — one click installs it.

Reliability

More reliable Windows updates

Updating on Windows is now completely hands-off. When a new version installs, Trade Studio reliably relaunches itself on your desktop instead of occasionally getting stuck after the update — so you always come back to a running app, with your bots picking up right where they left off.

June 2026 · v1.2.14
Reliability

Your bots keep running — and recover on their own

Three improvements to keep things running smoothly. Open positions and their protective stops now survive closing the app — state is saved safely, so shutting down can't corrupt it. If a bot ever stops unexpectedly, the app automatically restarts it in the background. And when you switch a bot between Classic and VWAP Reclaim, the watchlist and dashboard update instantly to match.

June 2026 · v1.2.13
New strategy

New strategy: VWAP Reclaim

Trade Studio now lets you choose how each bot trades. Alongside the original Classic Trend engine, there's a new intraday strategy called VWAP Reclaim. It waits for a stock to lose its session VWAP — the volume-weighted average price the market anchors to — then buys the reclaim when price pushes back above it inside an uptrend. Winners ride a wide, ATR-based target, and every position is flattened by the close, so you carry no overnight risk.

We selected it from research across roughly 130 strategies as the strongest intraday setup that still held up after realistic trading costs. It ships with its own curated, high-volatility watchlist — high-beta movers and liquid leveraged ETFs — because that's where the edge tends to live. Choose it per-bot in the strategy panel, with Patient, Balanced and Aggressive presets. It's long-only, so treat it as one to get to know on paper first — it's available now to paper trade.
