What is Trade Studio?

A desktop trading bot for Windows that runs on your computer and trades your Alpaca account — no servers, no sign-up, no data leaving your machine.

Trade Studio watches the market with a set of classic, transparent statistical signals — Bollinger Bands, RSI, MACD and volume on intraday candles. No AI black box, no hype: just rules you can reason about. When the signals line up, it places trades through your own Alpaca brokerage account and manages them with stop-losses and take-profits.

It's built to keep you in control. The bot boots paused, you can practice risk-free in paper mode for as long as you like, and your API keys are stored only on your machine — Trade Studio has no server and never sees your credentials. It's a tool, not a money machine.

Questions

Is it free?

Paper trading is free, forever — unlimited practice accounts, no card required. Live trading with real money is a paid subscription (Live Solo €9/mo, Live Plus €19/mo). You'll also need a free Alpaca brokerage account to connect.

Do my API keys leave my computer?

No. Your keys are saved only on your own machine (in your local app folder). There's no Trade Studio server and nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Will it make me money?

There are no guarantees. Trade Studio is a tool that runs a rules-based strategy — it is not financial advice and not a money machine. Trading carries real risk of loss. Start in paper mode.

What do I need to run it?

Windows 10 or 11 and a free Alpaca account. That's it — no Python, no setup beyond pasting your keys.

Is my money at risk in live mode?

Yes. Live mode places real orders with your real funds and can lose money. It's gated behind an explicit confirmation. Practice in paper mode first.

How do I stop it?

Pause it from the dashboard at any time, or quit entirely from the system-tray icon.
