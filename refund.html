Refunds & Right of Withdrawal

Last updated: 17 June 2026

EU/German 14-day right of withdrawal

As a consumer in the EU you normally have 14 days to withdraw from a digital purchase and get a refund — unless you have expressly agreed to immediate access and acknowledged that doing so forfeits that right. German law (BGB § 356(5)) requires this consent to be express.

Because a live subscription unlocks your license key immediately, we ask for that consent at checkout, with an unticked checkbox:

☐ I want access to my Trade Studio live license immediately, and I understand that by starting the subscription now I lose my 14-day right of withdrawal once access is provided.

We record that you ticked it, with a timestamp, against your order.

Our refund policy

  • Paper mode is free — nothing to refund.
  • If the withdrawal right has not been waived and you are within 14 days, you get a full refund.
  • After a valid waiver and immediate access, subscription fees are generally non-refundable, except where mandatory law says otherwise or at our discretion (e.g. a clear product fault you reported promptly).
  • Statutory warranty rights for defective digital products are unaffected.

Cancellation

Cancel any time from the Manage subscription link (Stripe billing portal). Cancellation stops future renewals; your license keeps working until the end of the period you already paid for, then live mode locks and paper mode stays free. No partial refunds for the remainder of a paid period unless required by law.
